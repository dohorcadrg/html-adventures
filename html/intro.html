Last day of class

It's the last day of CS222.

And like how every last class day is spent; you’re stuck doing a final.

You click answer after answer… Question number 65.. 66.. 67.. It’s a bit boring and complicated. And not to mention, BORING! Your mind begins to drift away, thinking of other things.

Finals are almost over. School is almost over. You can’t wait to get out of class. Winter break cannot come fast enough… You have so any plans, so many ideas.

But you continue on with the test.

68…69.. If only we can use the book, this is complicated.

70. The last question….

As you answer the last question on the exam, excitement and anticipation fill you. So close to freedom!

The test is done; and with a small click of the mouse, you submit your test. You glance around the room and see your fellow classmates still doing the test. You glance at the clock at the front side of the room. The minutes tick by slowly…

You have some time to kill. You can leave if you want, or stick around.

What do you do next?
