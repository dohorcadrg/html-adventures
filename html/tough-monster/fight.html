Just as the monster comes toward you, you hit him straight at the *CENSORED*.

Details are not needed, it’s not a pretty picture.

“Oww..” The monster cries. His voice was as deep as the ocean, but the cry as light as a feather in the breeze. And he did not just cry. But he cried like a little girl, even worse than the bunny previously did.

“I just wanted a hug!”

He continued crying like a little girl. You began to feel sorry for him.. Even though it was a bit weird.

“I’m sorry. ” You begin to apologize.

As soon as you uttered the words, the monster smiles the biggest smile a ten foot monster could smile, picks you up into his arms and cuddles you.

“I forgive you!!!!” He screams into your ear in happiness.

Well it seems all is well now…
