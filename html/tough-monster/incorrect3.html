Question 3 - Incorrect!

“Awwww!” The monster carries you once again and cuddles you into his arms..

At this point, the hugs are extremely annoying…. You stiffen up, full of irritation..

He ignores your reactions and continues to coddle you. "The <p> is not a value or an attribute..” He explains.

“Take a look at this”

He snaps his fingers and clicks his tounge, then a random piece of code materializes from thin air.

The HTML code hovered in the air.

“In this example, the href is that attribute. And the website.com is the value of that attribute.”

He place you down again gently, the motions reminded you of a person letting down a little puppy on the floor…

“Try to answer it again!”
