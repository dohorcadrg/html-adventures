Question 3 - Correct! You got the key!

“That’s right!” The monster leaps into the air in happiness and jumps down with forceful impact, causing a mini earthquake..

You try to hold on to the trembling earth, but the monster holds you steady until the ground stops quivering.

“Sorry.” He apologizes. “I’m just so happy you got the question right!” He reaches around and hands you a small brass key.. It looked miniature compared to his massive giant hands..

You reach for it and hold on to it. It’s surprisingly heavier than you expected it

The monster flashes a big toothy grin, and in that split second a blue teleportor pops up right next to you. You take a step towards it, ready for your next destination. Just as you place your foot against the blue metal, you hear a voice from behind.

“Goodbye!!” The monster says with a cheerfully happy, delicate voice.

You turn around to look at him.

His smile was as wide as a 10 foot scarcely monster could smile. “Good luck with getting the keys! I hope we can meet again one day! I’ll show you my unicorn collection! CUTE FLUFFY UNICORNS!!!!! ” He waved goodbye.

You waved goodbye, and gave an amused smile. The guy wasn’t that bad. He may be a little cuddly, but he means well. You nodded, satisfied with your thoughts. And in a flash, you teleport to your next destination.
