Question 2 - Incorrect!

“Awwww! Ywo gowt the question worwng!!!” The monster baby talked you and picked you up in his arms again, trying to console you…

You are not even upset…. The monster’s cuddly attitude is starting to get a bit annoying…

“Awre wou mad ywou gwot it wrowng?” The monster babbled into your ear

More like annoyed with being babied all the time, but you didn’t say a word.

“This question is a bit hard… But think of it this way, what does a TITLE do to a book? And can you see the title on a book?”

He placed you down and patted the top of your head with a giant finger…

“I’ll ask it again.. Think of a HTML title like a book title…”
