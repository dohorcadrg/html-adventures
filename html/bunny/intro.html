Into The World

You find yourself standing in a strange world.

All around, you see green binary code. They float around in patterns of on and off. The patterns of 1010101010101 fly around like regular birds. There are some other coding languages in the air, but the binary code is the most prominent.

Something about the way the world is structure reminds you of a computer.

As you glance around your surroundings, you notice a little white bunny bouncing around some 101010’s, chewing on the ones…

The seemingly normal bunny stands out from the sea of code. It seems like the only other biological life force around.

As you glance at it, the bunny twitches its tail and ears. It stops eating and turns around to meets your graze.

The cute bunny looks surprise for a bit. But then it twitches its nose and hops up to you in a non-threatening manner. As you noticed before it seems like a regular bunny, with floppy white ears and a cute little bow on its head.

“Awre ywou lowst?” The bunny speaks in a high pitch feminine voice, like a child’s. You didn’t expect her to talk, but you don’t dwell on the thought too much still processing the strangeness of the world. She gives a cute innocent smile; Her teeth shines as white as her fur.

What do you do say?
