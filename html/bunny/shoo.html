Shoo Bunny!

The bunny stays, seemly ignorant to what you said. “Shoooo?” The bunny questioned.

“GOOOO!” You move closer to the annoying rabbit, trying to chase it away.

The bunny holds fast to her position and tilts her ear to one side. Her long white floopy ear rests on the side of her head.

“Awre wyou mwad? “ The bunny asks with an innocent tone. "I down't wanna mwake wyou mwad" The bunny starts crying. "Pwealse don't gwet mad." She begins crying and crying and crying. But just as you thought that she'll cry until enternity; Her tears stop flowing and the bunny speaks with a happier note. “I cwan hewlp ywo gwet out! I cwan wmake wyou whappy agwain!”

You sighed half annoyed and half relieved. You might as well hear what the talking rodent had to say.
