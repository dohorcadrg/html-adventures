Deeper in the world

You reach your next destination, a random space filled with nothingness..

Except the stench of viniger..

As you take in your surroundings, (which was easy because it really was nothingness)

A random pickle jumps up to your attention.

“Is that pickle rick?” You asked. Yes, it was a stupid question but that was the first thing that popped into your mind.

“Nope it’s Pickle Nickle!!!!!!” The pickle screams, showing himself off.. “I’m a pickle! And I’m bigger than a nickel! I’M PICKLE NICKLE!!!”

“Uhh” You are at a lost for words. Should you laugh at a moving, breathing pickle? Or should you be afraid that there is such as thing as a moving breathing pickle?

As you think of your next reaction, Pickle Nickle taps his foot in impatience.

“Hey!” He yells, breaking you of your thoughts.

“You’re bugging me so just answer this question, get the key, and leave.”
