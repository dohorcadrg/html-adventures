HyperText Markup Language

Question 1 - Correct!

“Hmm” The pickle nodded, seemingly emotionless. “You actually got it right..” “<strong> denotes important text. <b> bolds text and <i> italizies text.<important> is not a real valid HTML tag.”

The pickle hummed, seemingly satisfied with your answer. He handed you the key you needed, even though it was dripped in pickle juice.

Just than the teleporter opened up below you, and activated without any of your input. The surroundings slowly started to melt away again…

Pickle Nickle gives a sly smile. “Good luck!” He gave a thumbs up with his pickle fingers. “You’re smarter than what you appear to be, kid.”

Did he actually compliment me? You questioned. Hey, this guy is probably not that bad after all.

Your surroundings vanish as you appear to your next destination.
