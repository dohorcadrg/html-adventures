Using the computer

You stayed inside to use the computer one last time. Why would you do that? You don’t know. It could be because you want to savor the moment. Savor the memories of your classmates. Remember the struggles of assignments, the joy of accomplishments. Remember the tears of the final..The moments of-

Or it could just be because you wanted to use the computer one last time. There is no hidden meaning behind it.

As you reached for the mouse, you felt a small shockwave send little electric bolts up your hand. Huh? That’s strange. You slowly reach for it again…. And zap!

In a moment you disappear from the room, completely gone.

You’re not in Northern Marianas College anymore.
