The Return Of The Bunny

You appear in the same room with the bunny. But this time, there was a large door behind her.

As the bunny smiles at you, the keys disappear from your hand and appear inside the keyholes of the door.

“Good job! You got the two keys! Now on to the last question and the last key, tehehe” Strangely, her cute accent was gone and she was speaking in perfect sentences. The bunny smiled sweetly, but with a hinge of creepiness. “This is a tricky one..” She started laughing, but instead of laughing in a cute innocent way, it sounded strangely sinister. “You better get this one…”

"Inline elements are normally displayed without starting a new line."
