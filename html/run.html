Run for freedom!!

You ran outside the room, screaming at the top of your lungs "Freedom!!!"

A few of your classmates glance at you as you zip past. You hear a voice tell you not to run.

But for this one moment in time, you ignore them and forget the rules. You feel free. Free as the wind during autumn..

And what sweet, sweet freedom it is. The feeling of winter break brings you back unto childhood. Childhood, a time of fewer responsibilities and more joys. Adulthood may have its charms of freedom. But there is a tough price to pay with more responsibilities. In both stages of life you are trapped in society. The exceptions and responsibilities..-

You get the point. For this one second of your life, you feel like there is one less burden and struggle. And just for a moment, you can be yourself..

As you contemplate over your newly founded freedom and your winter break plans, you noticed something different about your surroundings…

You’re not at Northern Marianas College anymore….
